Model Highlights

Let's go on a fast trip to show you the work achieved in The Model starting from The Designing Process going through The Construction Phase and a glance of The Final Shape.

The Designing Process

The Floor designs

Every floor is devided into two sections to fit the Laser Cutter Machine and get the best scale.

Walls

Every floor design illustrates the shape and includes tabs or the walls to fit into.

The Small Details

The Model can't be completed without the details that decorates it, chairs, desks and tables, even the stands that elevates the loors from each other.

The Construction

First things first, after the floor's two sections are printed the setions are sticked together to shape the full floor.

Secondly the walls are put into their tabs, and The stands that elevate the floors are sticked to the corners.

Finally after printing the four floors, every floor is put on the stands and they all are covered with a roof made of acrylic.
